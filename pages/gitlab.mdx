# Adding Gitsecure to Your GitLab Organization

Learn how effortlessly you can integrate Gitsecure into your GitLab organization.

![Gitsecure](/dashhome.png)

### Integrate Gitsecure into Your Organization

To integrate Gitsecure into your GitLab organization, follow these simple steps:

1. Click on the "Integrations" tab on your dashboard.
2. Locate GitLab integration as an option and click on the install button. 

![Gitsecure GitLab Integration](/gitlab.png)

### Generate an access token

Generate your GitLab personal access token using this [guide](https://gitlab.com/-/user_settings/personal_access_tokens?name=Gitsecure&scopes=api,read_api,read_user,read_repository,write_repository) and connect it to let GitSecure have an access to your GitLab projects.

Ensure, no expiry_date is set when creating the token! 
![GitLab access token](/access-token-gitlab.png)

### View Your Repositories

After connecting the Gitsecure app to GitLab, you will be redirected back to your Gitsecure dashboard. Here, you'll find a list of repositories that Gitsecure is monitoring.

![View Monitored Repositories](/gitlab-repo.png)
