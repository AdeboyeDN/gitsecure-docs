Learn how seamlessly you can get started with Gitsecure.

### 1. Create an Account on Gitsecure:

If you don't have an account on Gitsecure, follow these steps to create one [here](https://www.gitsecure.dev/auth/signup).

![Gitsecure Registration](/gsecreg.png)

Enter your organization/username, email address, and password, then click on the Signup button. A confirmation email will be sent to the provided email address. Click on the link within the email to proceed to the Gitsecure dashboard.

### 2. Login to Your Account:

If you already have an account on Gitsecure, simply log in to access the Gitsecure dashboard.

![Gitsecure Login](/gsecin.png)
