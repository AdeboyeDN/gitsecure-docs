Learn how to seamlessly it is to get started with Gitsecure.


### 1. Create an account on Gitsecure:
To begin with, if you don't have an account on Gitsecure, please create one [here](https://gitsecure.dev/auth/signup).

![Gitsecure](/gsecreg.png)

Enter your organization/username name, email address, and password, then click on the Signup button. A confirmation mail will be sent to the email address. Click on the link to continue to Gitsecure dashboard.

### 2. Login to your account 
If you already have an account on Gitsecure you can login to access Gitsecure dashboard
