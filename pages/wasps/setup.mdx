Learn how to seamlessly set up Wasps by following these simple steps.

### 1. Log in to Wasps:

To log into your Wasps account, follow these steps:
- Press `Cmd + Shift + P` (on Mac) or `Ctrl + Shift + P` (on Windows/Linux) to open the command palette in VS Code.
- Type `Wasps: API Key` and select it from the list.
- Alternatively, click on `Wasps (API Key)` on the tab below your terminal in VS Code.

![wasps demo](/api-key.png)

### 2. Get Your Wasps API Key:

To obtain your API key, you need to sign up on [Gitsecure](https://www.gitsecure.dev) and follow these steps:
- Sign up and log in to your Gitsecure account.
- Click on the **Settings** tab on your dashboard.
- Locate your API key in the settings. You also have the option to regenerate it if needed.
