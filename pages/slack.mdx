# Adding Gitsecure to Your Slack Workspace

Learn how effortlessly you can integrate Gitsecure into your Slack Workspace.

![Gitsecure](/dashhome.png)

### Integrate Gitsecure into Your Workspace

To integrate Gitsecure into your Slack workspace, follow these simple steps:

1. Click on the "Integrations" tab on your dashboard.
2. Locate Slack integration as an option and click on the install button.

![Gitsecure Slack Integration](/slack2.png)

### Select Your Slack Workspace and Channel

Once redirected to Slack, select the workspace and channel you want to integrate Gitsecure into by clicking on your preferred choice. You'll also be shown the permissions Gitsecure requires for effective alerts. After selecting your channel and accepting the permissions, proceed by clicking the "Allow" button.

![Select Slack Workspace](/slackmain.png)

### View Your Integration

After installing the Gitsecure Slack app, you will be redirected back to your Gitsecure dashboard. Here, you'll see that your slack integration is active and you will now get alerts in your slack channel.

![View Monitored Repositories](/slack1.png)

    Congratulations! You have successfully added Gitsecure to your Slack workspace.
